WomenTechies is Back!!

Greetings from DSC-VIT!

We hope you and your family are safe and healthy. This women’s day we at DSC-VIT are back with our annual women-centric hackathon WomenTechies to continue our vision to encourage and facilitate the participation of women in the tech space. Last year with an overwhelming number of participants and their innovative ideas, the hackathon was a hit because of you!


About WomenTechies’21 :

This 36-hour hackathon being held from 14th to 15th March aims to provide talented and independent women with the platform and resources to help them grow in their desired field and contribute to society with impactful and beneficial solutions. WomenTechies will be an opportunity for the budding developers to showcase their potential and skills under the mentorship of experts and come up with imperceptible ideas and ground-breaking solutions to problems that could potentially solve the woes of thousands of people.

With the event being made online to ensure the safety of our participants, this inclusive hackathon is undoubtedly going to be bigger and better this year.

Just keep in mind that your team should consist of a minimum of 50% female participants.

To learn more and register visit our website (dscv.it/wt21)

Hope to see you there!
